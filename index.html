Meet the Meridians! We can't wait to get married on our 11th anniversary and change our last name! We're looking forward to celebrating our special day with friends and family. All wedding links & resources can be found on this page! Keep scolling for more!

About Our Wedding

Some things about our special day will seem a bit non-traditional, but we will still be wearing white! Since we both identify as non-binary (neither male nor female) that means neither of us are a bride or groom! We'd like to be known as spouses, the happy couple, partners, or life partners. If you are tempted to say "bride" or "groom" please just use our first names instead. Also, neither of us will use "Mr & Mrs" rather we are "Mx & Mx" (before and after getting married.) When we get married, we are choosing a new family name "Meridian" in memory of the first street we lived on together. We ask that you please do not buy us gifts with gendered words on them. If you'd like to get us something special, feel free to print our new last name on any item or use M as a monogram. Again, we are so excited for all of our friends and family to join us in celebration. Thank you so much for understanding. Feel free to reach out to either of us if you have questions around this.
